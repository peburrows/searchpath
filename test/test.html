Searchpath

Finally there’s something better than outsourcing your blog search to Google. Searchpath features:

  • Fast, simple search for your site.
  • No ads.
  • User stays on your page, your domain.
  • Easy to add with a JavaScript snippet.
  • Stats for popular searches.
  • Also serves as a text backup for your site.


Innovative “popover” interface. Try it!

It’s as simple as pasting this wherever you want a search box:

<script type="text/javascript" src="http://js.searchpath.io/v1/yoursite.com"></script>

Searchpath will index your web site and make a search form available to your readers wherever you place the JavaScript tag.
